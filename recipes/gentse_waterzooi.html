Gentse Waterzooi

Description

General description of Gentse Waterzooi

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Cream
  • Potatoes

Preparation

  1. Chop up carrots, onions and garlic
  2. Boil all vegetables together
  3. Filet the chicken and add to the broth
  4. Prep the potatoes and boil them
