Beef Stew

Description

General description of beef stew

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • beef
  • Salt n pepper
  • Laurel
  • Potatoes
  • Dark beer

Preparation

  1. Chop up onions
  2. Melt utter and sear the beef
  3. Add spices while the meat cooks
  4. Add the onions and mix with the beer
  5. Simmer on a low fire for +/- 3 hours
